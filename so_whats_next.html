So, What's Next? Existential Risk (Spring 2023)

I decided to teach a class about the end of the world after reading Toby Ord's book The Precipice. I realized that a scientific examination of the apocalypse was possible and I set out to spread its fruits to the young people of the Boston Metropolitan Statistical Area. The class focused on Nick Bostrom's ontology of existential risk and the four main categories of it that people seemed to be worried about these days: nuclear war, climate change, runaway AI, and bioengineered pandemics. The slides for this 6-session class are located below.
